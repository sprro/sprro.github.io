#RESIST

Rogue Photographer is a project created by Jason Reynolds to help protect the environment and raise awareness about climate change. I donate 100% of the money I make from my photography to the National Resources Defense Council.

LEARN MORE

“Thousands of tired, nerve-shaken, over-civilized people are beginning to find out that going to the mountains is going home; that wildness is a necessity; and that mountain parks and reservations are useful not only as fountains of timber and irrigating rivers, but as fountains of life.”

- John Muir

Rogue Photographer was inspired by the individuals who created 'rogue' social media accounts for the government agencies of science in response to Donald Trump's anti-science administration. By raising awareness through the creative pursuit of photography, we can remind the deniers together that they cannot simply disregard facts, attempt to destroy environmental regulations and wreak havoc on an already fragile planet without the American people standing up to them.

Humanity has moral and ethical obligations to protect both the species of the planet and the poor from the increasing risks of climate change. By purchasing photos through Rogue Photographer, you're helping me to help the planet.

100% of profit made goes towards the National Resources Defense Council.

Get In Touch

Jason Reynolds is a full time designer and activist/creative photographer living in Seattle, WA. He's an avid rock-climber, snowboarder, lover of nature and all things science. In his free time he loves to hike, drink copious amounts of coffee, edit photos and harass family members about climate change. His hope is that he can help use Rogue Photographer to make an impact (big or small).

Jason doesn't work for the NRDC nor is he paid/affiliated with them. He just loves the planet... a lot.
